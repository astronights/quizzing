What is Quizzing?

Quizzing is more than just answering random trivia questions—it's an exhilarating journey that combines knowledge, creativity, and teamwork! Imagine standing on stage with your friends, working together to solve intriguing puzzles, tackle challenges, and compete for glory. Here’s what makes quizzing an exciting and unforgettable experience.

Where do I fit in?

I craft unique and exciting quiz formats that keep participants engaged and entertained. From traditional quizzes to interactive formats, I ensure every event is a fresh experience.

    Logistics of a Quiz

  • Audience

    50 - 200 people

  • Questions I've created

    60 - 80

  • Time

    2 - 4 hours

  • Team Size

    2 - 5 members

  • Audience

    I have hosted quizzes for schools, universities and for corporate audiences previously for 100+ participants.

  • Topics

    I can host quizzes on any topic you have. This could be a general quiz, Singapore themed or anything anything. More recently I hosted an egg themed quiz for an online community.

  • Venue

    With quizzing being super fun as a live event, all the venue requires is a seating capacity for the people along with a screen and a projector to display the quizzes on.

  • Format

    Standard quizzes have two rounds, a preliminary written round to select the top 6-8 teams. This is followed by a final round with the top teams battling it out on stage.
